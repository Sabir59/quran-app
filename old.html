Bring warmth and shelter to Gaza this Ramadan.

The last ten nights of Ramadan have arrived.

Within these nights lies Laylatul Qadr, a night better than a thousand months.

Now is the time to maximise the reward of your giving.

$200 will help distribute a Ramadan Family Food Pack to 2 families in Gaza.

For nearly two and a half years, we have watched the people of Gaza suffer immensely. Without the ummah stepping forward with humanitarian aid, their suffering would have only deepened. Their hardship continues this Ramadan.

In Gaza, this month is unfolding under hunger, displacement and uncertainty.

We cannot leave the people of Gaza to experience it alone.

Your support delivers hot meals, clean water, medical relief and shelter to families living in fragile conditions. Blankets and waterproof materials protect against rain and lingering cold. Meals restore strength. Water prevents illness. Aid on the ground is immediate and necessary.

If you feel you could have done more earlier in the month, the nights ahead remain an opportunity to provide.

We will not allow Gaza to go hungry.

For months, MATW teams have delivered 100,000 hot meals every weekend. During Ramadan, that effort has scaled to up to one million fresh meals for families in need, alongside emergency shelter and essential relief.

Be a source of hope for orphaned children going days without food. Stand beside widows struggling to care for their families. Support the elderly and vulnerable who cannot endure this alone.

Providing is a direct command from Allah SWT, Ar-Razaq, the All-Provider / Sustainer: “And spend [in the way of Allah] from what We have provided you.” [63:10]

Every blessing we hold is from Allah SWT, and nothing truly belongs to us. In this month of mercy, give from what He has entrusted to you.

The Prophet ﷺ said: “He is not a believer whose stomach is filled while his neighbour goes hungry.” (Sahih al-Bukhari)

Even when giving feels difficult, it strengthens our iman and becomes a means of relief for others.

This Ramadan, your Zakat and Sadaqah can bring real protection and relief to families in Gaza.

Give today.
